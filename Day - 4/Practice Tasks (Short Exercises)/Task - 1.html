Steps for making tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle or pot until it reaches the appropriate temperature.
  2. Place a tea bag or tea leaves into a cup or teapot.
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag or leaves.
  4. Let it steep for 2-5 minutes, depending on the type of tea and desired strength.
  5. Remove the tea bag or strain the leaves, then add sugar, milk, or lemon if desired, and enjoys your tea.
